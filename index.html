Our Story

The Calloway
Family Farm

Hollow Creek Farm was started in 1987 by Tom and Margaret Calloway on 40 acres of rolling Hudson Valley farmland. What began as a small roadside produce stand has grown into a year-round family destination.

Today, three generations of Calloways still work the land. We grow fresh fruits and vegetables, raise pasture-fed chickens, keep our own honeybees, and bake everything in our farm kitchen from scratch.

We believe a farm should feel like home. That's why we open our gates to visitors all year — for U-Pick days, hayrides, festivals, and quiet afternoons among the sunflowers.
