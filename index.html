About the Farm

Our Farm,
Our Promise

For more than 35 years, the Calloway family has grown fresh produce and farm-crafted goods with care, community, and sustainability at the heart of everything we do.

From field to store, we invite visitors to experience seasonal U-Pick harvests, farm dinners, and events set on our sunlit Hudson Valley property.

Every product in our store is made from ingredients grown on our land or sourced from trusted local partners we know by name.
